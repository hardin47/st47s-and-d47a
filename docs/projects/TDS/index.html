Teach Data Science

a data science blog - 2019 & 2020
Author

Hunter Glanz, Jo Hardin, and Nick Horton

In the summers of 2019 and 2020, Jo Hardin (Pomona College), Hunter Glanz (Cal Poly San Luis Obsipo), and Nick Horton (Amherst College) blogged daily about topics relevant to teaching data science. Below are a list of the blog topics with links to the entries. Of note, during the summer of 2020, the majority of entries were related to data science ethics.
